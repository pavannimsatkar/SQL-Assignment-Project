# 🛒 Retail Sales Analysis Using SQL

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing a retail sales dataset using SQL. The objective is to extract meaningful business insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, product popularity, payment preferences, and revenue generation.

Through this project, various SQL concepts such as filtering, sorting, aggregation, grouping, ranking, and reporting have been applied to solve real-world business problems.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze sales performance across different cities and categories.
* Identify top-performing products and categories.
* Understand customer purchasing behavior.
* Evaluate payment method preferences.
* Generate business reports using SQL.
* Practice SQL concepts through 150+ analytical queries.

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains retail transaction records with the following attributes:

| Column Name   | Description                   |
| ------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Sale_ID       | Unique transaction identifier |
| Customer_Name | Customer name                 |
| Gender        | Gender Category               |
| Age           | Customer age                  |
| City          | Customer city                 |
| Category      | Product category              |
| Product_Name  | Product purchased             |
| Payment_Mode  | Payment method used           |
| Quantity      | Units purchased               |
| Price         | Price per unit                |
| Rating        | Customer rating               |
| Order_Date    | Date of purchase              |

## 📊 Key Business Insights

### Revenue Performance

The business generated significant revenue through product sales, with Electronics emerging as the highest-performing category. Revenue analysis indicates that a large portion of sales is driven by high-value products.

### City Performance

Pune was identified as the top revenue-generating city, followed by Mumbai and Jaipur. These locations contribute the majority of overall business revenue.

### Customer Behavior

Customers above the age of 25 generated a considerable share of sales revenue. This customer segment appears to have stronger purchasing power and contributes significantly to overall business growth.

### Product Performance

Electronics products consistently achieved the highest sales value and revenue contribution. Product demand analysis revealed strong customer preference for technology-related products.

### Payment Trends

Digital payment methods such as UPI and Card were the most preferred transaction options. Cash payments accounted for a smaller percentage of overall transactions.

### Customer Satisfaction

The average customer rating remained above 4 out of 5, indicating a positive customer experience and overall satisfaction with products and services.
